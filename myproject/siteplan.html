Overberg WildFire Tracking

This site will be used to track and report wildfires within the Overberg region. The main features of the site will include:

  • A map displaying current wildfire locations using an API.
  • A form for users to report new wildfires with details such as location, size, and status.
  • A database to store reported wildfire data.
  • Weather information relevant to wildfire conditions.
  • Resources and safety tips for residents in wildfire-prone areas.

The site will be designed with a focus on usability and accessibility, ensuring that users can easily navigate and find the information they need.

Scenarios

Residents wanting to know dangerzones to avoid

Firefighters needing to report new fires
